Problem statement
In a Binary Search Tree (BST), which property is true?

Options: Pick one correct answer from below
• The left child of a node is greater than the node.
• The right child of a node is less than the node.
• The left child of a node is less than the node, and the right child is greater than the node.
• The left child of a node is greater than the node, and the right child is less than the node.

ans:
The left child of a node is less than the node, and the right child is greater than the node.
