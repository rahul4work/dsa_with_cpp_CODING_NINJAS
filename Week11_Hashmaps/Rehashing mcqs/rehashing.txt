Problem statement
Consider a hashmap with an initial bucket size of 10. Currently, 
7 elements have been inserted into the hashmap. If we insert an 8th element, 
what will be the resulting count (number of elements) and bucket size?

Options: Pick one correct answer from below
• Count: 20, Bucket size : 20
• Count: 8, Bucket size: 20
• Count: 10, Bucket size: 10
• Count: 8, Bucket size: 10

ans: Count: 8, Bucket size: 20
