Problem statement
Which of the following statements about operator overloading in C++ is true?


Options: One or more answers may be correct
• All operators can be overloaded in C++.
• Operator overloading requires modifying the compiler.
• Overloaded operators must take at least two operands.
• The number of operands cannot be changed for overloaded operators.


answer:-
The number of operands cannot be changed for overloaded operators.
